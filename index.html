Find Out More

Our mission is to provide the finest indoor, secured, self-storage facility for the car enthusiast.

A storage facility without temperature control and proper ventilation can damage your investment. You no longer have to store your valuable vehicle in the local "U-Store-Um" with boats, furniture, mobile homes, and who knows what.

CARS offers a unique, indoors, highly secured automobile storage facility that is temperature-controlled with proper ventilation and fire protection.

This facility is specifically designed for the car enthusiast to safely store your investment and provide a place to gather with fellow car enthusiasts, exchange stories and develop new friendships.

All The Features You Want, All In One Place.

The facility is 30,000 square feet enclosed, weather-tight, highly secured, and single purpose. We only store vehicles. Whether it's a classic, antique, race or specialty car or truck, we store it. The facility is heated and air cooled with an air-conditioned office and meeting room.
